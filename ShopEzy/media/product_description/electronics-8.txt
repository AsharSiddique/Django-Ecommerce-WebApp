Display: The watch features a vibrant AMOLED display with customizable watch faces.

Sizes: It is available in two sizes: 40mm and 44mm.

Design: The watch has a stainless steel or aluminum case with various color options.

Performance: It is equipped with Samsung's Exynos W920 dual-core processor, offering smooth performance.

Health and Fitness: The Galaxy Watch 4 includes advanced health and fitness tracking capabilities, including heart rate monitoring, blood pressure monitoring, ECG app, sleep tracking, and a wide range of workout tracking options.

Connectivity: It supports Wi-Fi and Bluetooth connectivity and can be paired with Android smartphones as well as iPhones.

Battery Life: The battery life of the Galaxy Watch 4 can last up to 2 days on a single charge, depending on usage.
