Display: The Series 7 has an OLED display with thinner bezels compared to previous models.

Sizes: It is available in two sizes: 41mm and 45mm.

Design: The watch has an aluminum or stainless steel case with various color options.

Performance: It is powered by Apple's S7 dual-core processor for improved performance.

Health and Fitness: The Series 7 includes various health and fitness features such as heart rate monitoring, ECG app, blood oxygen level measurement, sleep tracking, and a range of workout tracking options.

Connectivity: It supports Wi-Fi and Bluetooth connectivity, allowing you to pair it with your iPhone or other compatible devices.

Battery Life: The battery life of the Series 7 can last up to 18 hours on a single charge.
